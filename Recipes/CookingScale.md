Cooking Scale
=============

A lot of my recipes use weight, usually grams.  Cooking scales are really inexpensive, extremely accurate, and super-convenient to use: just put your mixing bowl on it, hit the tare button, add each ingredient to the weight needed, hitting tare between each.

No more fussing with measuring cups and spoons (or having to wash them!)  It's really easy to scale recipies or adjust them by small amounts.  And the results are very reproducible; I even make my morning coffee by weight.

Just get one, ~10lb / 5Kg capacity, 0.1g accuracy, about $20 on Amazon; avoid any with reviews that say it's slow to update, you want it to be responsive.

If you have recipes that use volume measurements, make them once or twice on the scale, adding the ingredients and writing down the weight (rounding or adjusting as needed) to convert them.
